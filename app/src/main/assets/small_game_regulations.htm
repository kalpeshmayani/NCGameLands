-    GENERAL RESTRICTIONS

o Field possession limit while hunting is the same as the daily bag limit.

o No person shall possess more than one daily bag limit while in the field or while returning from the field to one’s vehicle, hunting camp or residence.

o Axes and saws may not be carried when raccoon or opossum hunting.

o It is unlawful to shoot raccoon during the daytime, west of U.S. 1, except in Richmond County.

o It is unlawful to carry firearms or climbing irons when training dogs during closed seasons, except under special field-trial regulations.

-    GROUNDHOG,NUTRIA, COYOTE,SKUNK (STRIPED), ARMADILLOAND FERAL SWINE (HOGS)

o There is no closed season or bag limit. Hunters may use electronic calls for coyotes and feral swine.

o Feral swine may be hunted at night with lights.

o Coyotes may be hunted at night except in the counties of Beaufort, Dare, Hyde, Tyrrell and Washington. In those five counties, the following apply:

§ Hunting hours are 1/2 hour before sunrise to 1/2 hour after sunset

§ All coyote hunting requires a permit

§ Permits can be obtained at ncwildlife.org or by calling 1-888-248-6834

§ All coyote harvests must be reported.
